# SchoolERP AI Suite: Intelligence with Guardrails

Stop drowning in repetitive queries and manual data entry. Our AI Suite is designed specifically for schools—grounded in your data, safe for your students, and always under your control.

---

## 1. AI Parent Helpdesk (24/7)
**Problem**: Front desk staff spend 4 hours/day answering "When is the exam?" or "What are the fees?".
**Solution**: An intelligent chatbot on WhatsApp and Web that answers instantly using **only** your official circulars.
-   **Safe**: Won't hallucinates dates. If it doesn't know, it escals to staff.
-   **Multilingual**: Speaks Hindi, English, and regional languages fluently.

## 2. Teacher Assistant
**Problem**: Great lesson plans take hours to draft.
**Solution**: Generate a 3-day lesson plan + quiz for "Photosynthesis, Class 6" in 30 seconds.
-   **Teacher-in-the-loop**: AI drafts, Teacher reviews and finalizes.
-   **Aligned**: Maps to CBSE/ICSE curriculum topics.

## 3. Fee Intelligence
**Problem**: Identifying who *won't* pay on time is guesswork.
**Solution**: Predictive models analyze payment history to flag "At-Risk" accounts before the due date.
-   **Actionable**: Suggests gentle reminders for specific parents.
-   **Cashflow**: Improves on-time collection by 20%.

## 4. Voice-to-Text Remarks
**Problem**: Typing incident reports or student diaries on a phone is tedious.
**Solution**: Speak naturally—"Rohan was excellent in the debate today"—and let AI transcribe and file it.
