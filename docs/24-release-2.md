# Release 2: Operations & Specialized Modules

## Overview
Release 2 expands the SchoolERP foundation with specialized modules for school operations, logistics, and professional networking.

## Scoped Modules

### 1. Transport Management
- **Status**: Complete
- **Features**: 
    - Vehicle and Driver registration.
    - Route planning and Stop sequencing.
    - Student allocation to routes.
    - Automated transport fee integration.

### 2. Library Management
- **Status**: Complete
- **Features**:
    - Cataloging for physical books and digital assets (PDF/EPUB).
    - Issue/Return workflow with due date management.
    - Automatic fine calculation for overdue returns.

### 3. Inventory & Procurement
- **Status**: Complete
- **Features**:
    - Category-based item management.
    - Supplier database.
    - Purchase Order (PO) workflows.
    - Stock level tracking and adjustments.

### 4. Admission Pipeline
- **Status**: Complete
- **Features**:
    - Public enquiry form for lead generation.
    - Lead tracking and status updates.
    - Detailed application forms with document attachments.
    - Recording and tracking processing fees.

### 5. HRMS & Payroll
- **Status**: Moved to Phase 3 (In Progress)
- **Features**:
    - Employee lifecycle management.
    - Salary structure configuration (Basic, HRA, DA, Allowances).
    - Payroll run processing.
    - Generate and view payslips.

### 6. Alumni & Career
- **Status**: Moved to Phase 3 (In Progress)
- **Features**:
    - Alumni database with batch/year tracking.
    - Placement drive management.
    - Job applications for alumni.

## Integration
- All modules utilize the central **Audit Log** and **Policy Engine**.
- Multi-tenancy is strictly enforced across all 12 modules.
